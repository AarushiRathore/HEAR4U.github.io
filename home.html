What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. It also helps determine how we handle stress, relate to others, and make choices. Mental health is very important at every stage of life, from childhood and adolescence through adulthood.“Our mental health is something that impacts our eating and sleeping habits and has a lot to do with our physical health as well. It is important to address our mental health needs, or oftentimes we will feel the impact of it in other areas of our health as well.”

Why is Mental Health Important?

Good mental health helps individuals cope with life's challenges, work productively, and build healthy relationships.Mental health is crucial for overall well-being, enabling individuals to cope with life's stresses, build relationships, and contribute to their communities, impacting everything from daily tasks to long-term goals. Foundation for Overall Health: Mental health is an integral part of overall well-being, alongside physical health and social well-being.

Common Mental Health Disorders

  • Depression
  • Anxiety Disorders
  • Bipolar Disorder
  • Schizophrenia
  • Post-Traumatic Stress Disorder (PTSD)

Tips for Mental Well-being

  • Practice mindfulness and meditation
  • Exercise regularly
  • Eat a balanced diet
  • Get enough sleep
  • Stay connected with loved ones
