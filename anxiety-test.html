Anxiety Test

1. Do you feel nervous or on edge?

2. Do you find it difficult to control your worry?

3. Do you have trouble relaxing?

4. Do you feel restless and can't sit still?

5. Do you feel easily annoyed or irritated?

6. Do you feel afraid as if something awful might happen?

Note: This test is not a diagnostic tool. Please consult a healthcare professional for a thorough assessment.
