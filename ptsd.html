PTSD Test

1. Have you experienced nightmares or unwanted memories of the traumatic event?

2. Do you feel numb or detached from people, activities, or your surroundings?

3. Do you feel constantly on guard, watchful, or easily startled?

4. Do you avoid certain places, people, or activities that remind you of the traumatic event?

5. Do you experience difficulty concentrating or sleeping?

6. Do you experience negative thoughts or feelings about yourself or others?

Note: This test is not a diagnostic tool. Please consult a healthcare professional for a thorough assessment.
